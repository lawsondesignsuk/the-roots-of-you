About Me

Here to help you feel better

My name is Rebecca, and I am based in the foothills of Malvern. I am a Reiki and Swedish Massage Therapist, specialising in hot stone therapy. My holistic approach ensures each treatment is tailored to individual needs, recognising that well-being challenges often have multiple roots.


As a member of the UK Reiki Federation and FHT (Federation of Holistic Therapies), I adhere to strict ethical codes and continuously develop my skills to maintain high standards of care. Additionally, the clinic where I practise requires all therapists to be DBS-checked, so you can rest assured you're in safe hands.
